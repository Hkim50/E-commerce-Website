ABOUT US

Kicks 'n Kits Club is driven by a team of four ordinary people who love and appreciate sports and the culture behind it. Our goal is to expand the love of the culture of sport shoes and sport kits. Each and every shoe and jersey has a story and wearing it allows you to express and share that story with others. From Michael Jordan's shoes that he played in when he had the flu, to the jersey that Cristiano Ronaldo wore when he score his first goal letting the world know a new star has been born.


We love the expression that shoes and jerseys allows. We also love the story that comes with it. So we invite you to embark in this adventure with us!
